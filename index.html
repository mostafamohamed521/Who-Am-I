How to Play

Guess Who is a deduction game. Ask yes/no questions about physical traits to identify a hidden character.

🎮 DIFFICULTY LEVELS:

⭐ EASY - AI is slow and makes random choices. Easy to win!
⭐ MEDIUM - AI is balanced. Fair challenge!
⭐ HARD - AI is super fast and intelligent. Very challenging!

🎮 GAME FEATURES:

⭐ 10 AI Algorithms - CSP, Backtracking, Minimax, Alpha-Beta, Best-First, Heuristic, Local Search, Genetic, Bio-Inspired, Uninformed BFS
⭐ Timer System - You have 30 seconds per turn!
⭐ Score System - Earn points for correct guesses
⭐ Save/Load - Save your game and continue later

📖 HOW TO PLAY - MODE 1:

1. Select your character and color
2. Take turns asking yes/no questions about physical traits
3. Click on character cards to eliminate candidates
4. Enable GUESS MODE and click a character to make your final guess
5. Wrong guesses will pass the turn to AI and cost you a guess
