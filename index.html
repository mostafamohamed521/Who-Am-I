How to Play

Guess Who is a deduction game. Ask yes/no questions about physical traits to identify a hidden character.

MODE 1 — YOU VS AI

Both you and the AI secretly select a character. Take turns asking questions. To eliminate candidates during your turn, click on character cards in your board. To make a final guess, enable Guess Mode, then click on a character.

MODE 2 — AI GUESSES

You select a secret character. Choose an algorithm and watch the AI deduce step by step.
